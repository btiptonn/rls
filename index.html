üìò User Guide

  • Scan your RFID tag to unlock or claim the washer.
  • Once the LED turns green, select a cycle time using the buttons.
  • The machine automatically detects movement using an internal vibration sensor.
  • If no movement is detected for too long, the washer will abort and turn red.
  • When the laundry finishes, the LED turns blue. The washer enters lock mode.
  • You are asked to scan your RFID again to scan out and unlock the machine.
  • If the previous user forgets to scan out, another user can override the lock by holding their RFID on the reader for 3 seconds until the LED flashes override mode.
  • While locked, the LED stays orange.
  • When idle with no activity, the LED stays white.
