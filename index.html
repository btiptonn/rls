How to Use

1. Press the **START** button on the washer Pico.

2. The LED will turn **blue** → scan your RFID card.

3. When accepted, LED turns **yellow** and sends data.

4. During washing, LED stays **green**.

5. When finished, LED returns to **white**.

6. Press the **RESET** button if you need to stop early.

Note: This website lets the owner monitor who used the washer.
